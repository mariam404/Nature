Tips to start your journey safely
in Forests

Stay Safe in the Forest
  1. Travel with a companion.
  2. Be in good physical condition.
  3. Think about your footing while traveling near cliffs.
  4. Wear appropriate clothing for the trail conditions and season.

Tips to start your journey safely
in Desert

Stay Safe in the Desert
  1. Make sure you have sufficient food rations and water.
  2. One gallon of water, per person, per day is the absolute minimum.
  3. Dress for it: wear light-colored, loose fitting clothes.
  4. Always pack a hat!

Tips to start your journey safely
in Mountains

Stay Safe in the Mountains
  1. Plan your route properly.
  2. Adapt your plans to the circumstances.
  3. Bring lots of water!
  4. Cover your head, and apply sunscreen at regular intervals.
